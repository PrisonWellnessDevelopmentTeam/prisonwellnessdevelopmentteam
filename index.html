Vision and Mission Statements

    Vision Statement

  • By introducing skills unique to the practice of Occupational Therapy, we will lead the way in implementing sensory-based interventions into correctional facilities to promote positive outcomes of staff-offender interactions and to increase correctional staff job satisfaction. We will continue to expand the foundation of evidence in this area of practice through providing therapy within frameworks specific to the practice of occupational therapy and producing measurable outcomes through standardized/non-standardized assessment.
